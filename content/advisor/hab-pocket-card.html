HAB · BATTLE REFERENCE
Bosses, Recovery & KPIs
Objections are uncertainty wearing a costume.

The 8 Boss Battles — tell → move

B1 “I'll sell the car”Reframe vs. used-car cost + unknown history.
B2 “Price too high”"We're not cheapest, intentionally." Anchor protection.
B3 “No money”= "no money TODAY." Pivot to financing, stay on line.
B4 “Talk to spouse”Three-way bypass. Get them on the call now.
B5 “I'll wait”Safety: name risk concretely. Maint: value reframe.
B6 “Always selling”Pro Obligation frame. Fiduciary, not salesperson.
B7 “Need car today”Solve transport FIRST. Then return to repair.
B8 “Parts & labor?”Refuse apples-to-apples. Sell protection program.

5 Universal Objection Rules

  1. Never argue. Win the argument, lose the customer.
  2. Acknowledge first, always. "I completely understand."
  3. Find the real concern. Clarifying question, then respond.
  4. Pivot to value or risk, never to discount.
  5. Re-ask for the close. Every exchange ends moving forward.
